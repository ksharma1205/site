Biography

Lord Djinnx is a musician from Tampa Florida who produces experimental electronic music as well as hip hop instrumentals. He loves almost every genre and his favorite artists are Radiohead, Aphex Twin, Flying Lotus, Kendrick Lamar, Elliott Smith, and Flatbush Zombies just to name a few. He began producing in 2015 and began selling merchandise such as cd's and clothing. He has created 5 mixtapes that have resonated in his local area. As well as producing Lord Djinnx loves reading and djing. Check out Lord Djinnx's music today!
